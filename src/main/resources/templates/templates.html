About us

Being part of Latvian underground techno scene for over a decade, we believe, that there is a lot of unrealised potential there, just sitting and waiting to be exposed.

Exposed Records is an independent record label aiming at uncovering the best our artists have to offer, conquering bureaucracy and taking it beyond the "easy" digital domain.
